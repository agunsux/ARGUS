## Severity Levels

| Level | Definition | Response Time |
|-------|-----------|---------------|
| 🔴 Critical | Sistem down, data loss | < 15 menit |
| 🟡 Major | Fitur utama terganggu | < 1 jam |
| 🟢 Minor | Gangguan non-kritis | < 24 jam |

## Response Procedure

1. **Identify** — Deteksi dan konfirmasi insiden
2. **Triage** — Tentukan severity level
3. **Contain** — Batasi dampak
4. **Resolve** — Implementasi fix
5. **Postmortem** — Dokumentasi evidence-010
